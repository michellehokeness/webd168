HELLO,

My name is Michelle Hokeness. I am a Hair Stylist, a Web Developer, and Graphic Designer living in the beautiful country town of Fallbrook that is just on the outskirts of San Diego, California.

About me

I have been a professional hairstylist for the past 30 years. I started dabbling in Web Development in 2020 when I wasn't able to work in the salon due to Covid19. My son is a software engineer and thought I might enjoy it. He was right! It is a completely different way of being creative and I like being able to do it on a computer device as opposed to touching humans. It definitely was a nice change.

I am taking WEBD168 - Intermediate HTML and CSS at present. I love hard coding and want to get better at it. Another reason for taking this class is that it fills a requirement for the Web Development Program for an associate's degree at Mesa Community College and I am currently working towards that. I hope to land a job in web development or design and start a new career. I will miss the relationships I have with my hair clients but I am ready to earn a living being creative in a different way. Programs I have completed:

  • Full Stack Web Development SD Continuing Ed
  • Graphic Design SD Continuing Ed
  • Content Management Systems - WordPress

In my leisure time I enjoy...
