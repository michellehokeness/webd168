HELLO,

My name is Michelle Hokeness. I am a Hair Stylist, a Web Developer, and Graphic Designer living in the beautiful country town of Fallbrook that is just on the outskirts of San Diego, California.

About me

I have been a professional hairstylist for the past 30 years. I started dabbling in Web Development in 2020 when I wasn't able to work in the salon due to Covid19. My son is a software engineer and thought I might enjoy it. He was right! It is a completely different way of being creative and I like being able to do it on a computer device as opposed to touching humans. It definitely was a nice change.

I am taking WEBD168 - Intermediate HTML and CSS at present. I love hard coding and want to get better at it. Another reason for taking this class is that it fills a requirement for the Web Development Program for an associate's degree at Mesa Community College and I am currently working towards that. I hope to land a job in web development or design and start a new career. I will miss the relationships I have with my hair clients but I am ready to earn a living being creative in a different way. Programs I have completed:

  • Content Management Systems - WordPress

In my leisure time I enjoy...

spending time with the family. My family includes my husband, my mother (lives with us), our 2 dogs, and our 4 cats - 3 are black and one is a Maine Coon. We have a son and 3 daughters and 5 grandchildren but they all live in other states. We enjoy spending our time with them as well whenever they are in town. I also enjoy watching movies on the big screen and in the comfort of my own home. Whenever I see a movie on the big screen I have to watch it while also enjoying popcorn and red vines.

I love to run. I am competative and love to run in races such as the 5k, 10k, half marathon and marathon. An ultra marathon (a race that is more than a marathon - usually 50k or more is considered an ultra marathon) is definitely on my bucket list. There are always a variety of goals I can set for myself whether they are for a specific time or distance or both, running gives me a variety of ways to challenge myself and have fun while helping me improve my fitness - of mind and body.

I really enjoy coming up with new ideas to create in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. Any reason to open up those two is a good day for me. The image in this about section is something I would have normally created in Illustrator but I decided to try out Figma. It was a nice experience and I am glad I did it. This image is a representation of me and one of my cats.

I also enjoy reading books. I love a good crime mystery like "The girl with the Dragon Tattoo" series, by Stieg Larsson and historical fiction like "A Reliable Wife", by Robert Goolrick. I have also been enjoying reading memoirs like, "Choosing to Run", by Des Linden. When I am running a lot of miles during the week I will listen to books on Audible. It's a great way to relax and "read" during my easy runs.
